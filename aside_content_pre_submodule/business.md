<h3>Overview</h3>
<p>This dashboard provides business stakeholders with access to the platform vision, product roadmap, token economy documentation, and strategic guidance for the Town Ruins platform.</p>
</div>

<div class="tr-dashboard-section">
<h3>Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Align platform development with the documented vision and roadmap</li>
<li>Understand the TR Token economy and its impact on monetization</li>
<li>Review temporary stay policies and business rules</li>
<li>Guide product decisions using the feature roadmap and strategy documents</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="tr-dashboard-section">
<h3>Business Rules</h3>
<ul>
<li>Money is only used to purchase TR Tokens, except for temporary stay bookings</li>
<li>Every premium platform feature consumes TR Tokens</li>
<li>Token payer roles and billing semantics are defined in <a href="./business/token_economy">Token Economy</a></li>
<li>Temporary stay booking payments use real payment provider flows</li>
</ul>
