# User Manual

## For tenants

Tenants can browse rental listings, search for temporary stays, save searches, and contact landlords. When a landlord approves an engagement, the tenant can see the landlord contact details and the engagement fee is charged in TR Tokens.

## For landlords

Landlords can create and manage rental listings, restore expired listings with TR Tokens, and respond to tenant engagement requests.

## For providers

Providers can manage accommodations, rooms, availability, pricing, and booking-related operations.

## For administrators

Administrators can moderate listings, manage providers, review disputes and reports, and oversee legal documents.
