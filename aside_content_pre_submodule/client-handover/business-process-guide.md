# Business Process Guide

## Core business flow

1. A landlord creates a listing and the listing enters the lifecycle supported by the backend.
2. A tenant can browse listings and contact a landlord through an engagement flow.
3. When the landlord approves the engagement, the tenant is charged 5 TR Tokens and the engagement status changes to approved.
4. Providers can manage temporary stays and booking-related operations through the provider experience.

## Current platform rules

- Listing creation and restoration flows are token-based in the current implementation.
- Temporary stay bookings are a distinct flow from the long-term rental listing flow.
- Admin moderation and settlement actions are present in the current repository but their operational procedures depend on the deployed environment.
