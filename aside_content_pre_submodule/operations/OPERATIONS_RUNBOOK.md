## Scope

This runbook captures the current operational workflow for the Amplify frontend and Render-backed backend. It intentionally avoids unverified infrastructure details.

## Current Operational Activities

- Monitor sign-in, verification, and booking flows.
- Review moderation and admin actions.
- Confirm uploads and notifications continue to function.
- Re-run smoke checks after release changes.
